Publications

  • Love Mehta and Abhinav Dhall. Fast Face and Saliency Aware Collage Creation for Mobile Phones. Accepted in 13th IEEE Automatic Faces Gesture Recognition 2018 (FG18), China, 15-19 May 2018.
    2018

    Developed an android app that creates semantically meaningful collages from images based on faces, image saliency and hybrid blending. The algorithm is designed for computational efficiency inorder for it to run on a mobile device. To the best of our knowledge this is one of the first mobile hardware optimised applications, which considers the faces and the region importance and later merges the salient images automatically in a non-rigid grid layout. Other interesting works either have a semi-rigid or a fully rigid grid based layout or are desktop based.

  • Aamir Mustafa, Amanjot Kaur, Love Mehta and Abhinav Dhall. Predicting Student Engagement in the Wild. Under Review in 24th IEEE International Conference on Pattern Recognition (ICPR2018), China, 20-24 August 2018.
    2017-2018

    A new dataset is created to study the user engagement in e-learning environments. We propose a new engagement database, which has been recorded in diverse conditions. Deep multiple instance learning framework is learnt for engagement prediction and for localization of engaging and non-engaging parts of the stimuli video. Dataset is manually annotated by the labelers and the predicted labels of the videos are correlated with the manually annotated videos.

  • Sai Samarth R Phaye, Love Mehta and Mukesh Saini. The One Man Show. Accepted in 19th IEEE International Symposium on Multimedia (ISM2017), Taiwan, 11-13 December 2017.
    2017-2018

    Developed a Android/MATLAB application that enables the user to record different solo performance videos and merge them in synchronization. Proposed a novel method, Dominant Audio - Video Production, for sequencing and cutting the videos in an intuitive manner to artificially create a human like movie making experience. Used Machine Learning (SVM / ANN) and Multimedia Processing Techniques for instrument based classification of videos and enhancing them individually.
